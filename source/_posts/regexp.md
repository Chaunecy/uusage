## 前瞻、后顾

```bash
echo "Here is a string, and Here is another" | grep -o -P "(?<=Here).*(?=string)" 
# is a string, and Here is another #greedy
echo "Here is a string, and Here is another" | grep -o -P "(?<=Here).*?(?=string)" 
# is a 
# is another
```
